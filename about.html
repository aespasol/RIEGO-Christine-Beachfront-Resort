Welcome to Beachfront

Welcome To Our Resort

Start an amazing adventure at our cozy beachfront resort on the charming island of Polo in Santa Cruz, Marinduque. This website is like your ticket to discover the untouched beauty of our growing paradise – your way to experience real island life. Come along as we show you the peaceful charm of Beachfront Resort, ready to welcome those who love adventure to a calm and cozy spot.

Step onto Polo's shores with us, where every wave has a new story to tell, and each sunset is like a beautiful painting on the growing picture of Beachfront Resort. Your one-of-a-kind island getaway starts right here, where nature and friendly service come together to make an unforgettable escape.

Cottage

Various cottage options are available for rental, spanning from cement cottages to those made of semi-bamboo cottages.

Store

In the meantime, while the restaurant is still under construction and is set to open soon, there is a convenience store available at the resort.

Transportation

The fee for boat transportation from Buyabod Port to the Beachfront Resort amounts to ₱50.00 per individual.

Videoke

Videoke is offered for rent as well, with a rental fee of ₱700.00.
